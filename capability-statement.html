Who We Are

Lot 9 LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business founded in October 2022. The company began serving the veteran community in e-commerce, expanded into local service operations, and moved into information technology through a managed IT and live-streaming partnership that remains active today. We are now focused on business-to-government technology resale — supplying federal agencies with IT hardware, software, communications, and medical equipment through authorized distribution channels.

Our value is straightforward: agencies get a responsive, veteran-owned partner that meets socio-economic contracting goals, and we bring the catalogs of major OEMs and national distributors into set-aside channels those large firms cannot bid directly.
